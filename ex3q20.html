PROJECT


SOUL FOOD (Mess Food Management System)

The purpose of this Android Application is to reduce food wastage. This app is mainly designed for Banasthali students to get the information about the meal just at a click.

In many messes, there is no facility to see the menu and then mark their attendance according to their choice which will reduce food wastage.

Here we are providing features of marking attendance and also to see today’s menu. It would be possible to reduce the food wastage in mess by marking attendance and without using much efforts and manpower if, there existed a software for the same.
